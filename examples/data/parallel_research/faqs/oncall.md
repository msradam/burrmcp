# On-call FAQ

## I just got paged. What now?

Acknowledge within 5 minutes. Open an incident channel. Post initial
summary. See the incident-response runbook for the full protocol.

## How do I escalate?

Page the secondary via PagerDuty's escalate button. For P0 incidents
also page the engineering manager on-call.

## Can I take a long lunch during my on-call shift?

Yes, but: stay within 15 minutes of a laptop, and let your team know in
the on-call channel before you step away.

## What if I miss a page?

After 10 minutes without ack, PagerDuty pages the secondary
automatically. Apologize, take the page back if appropriate, and follow
up in the on-call retro.

## Where's my swap calendar?

The on-call rotation schedule is in PagerDuty under "Production
On-Call". Swaps are coordinated in the `#oncall-swaps` channel.
